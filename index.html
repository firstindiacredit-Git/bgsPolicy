Privacy Policy for BESTGOOGLESITE Extension

We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal data is collected, used, and protected when you use the BESTGOOGLESITE extension.

Data Collection

The BESTGOOGLESITE Chrome Extension does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The extension only modifies the new tab page in Google Chrome to redirect users to a custom homepage URL specified by the user.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • chrome.tabs: Used to interact with browser tabs and change the new tab page to the custom URL.
  • chrome.storage: Used to store and sync the custom homepage URL across devices.

No Tracking or Data Sharing

The extension does not track your activity, collect browsing data, or share data with any external servers, except for redirecting you to the custom homepage URL you have specified.

User Rights

Since the extension does not collect personal data, there is no data for you to access, modify, or delete. You can simply uninstall the extension if you no longer wish to use it.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected here on this page.
